[anādinidhanaṃ śāntaṃ bhāvābhāvavivarjitam|nirvikalpaṃ nirālambamanavasthitamadvayam

adṛṣṭāntamanākhyānamacintyamanidarśanam|anāśrayāpratiṣṭhānaṃ nirvikāramasaṃskṛtam

sarveṣāmāśrayaṃ buddhaṃ karuṇāmayavigraham|nānādhimuktasattvānāṃ nānopāyapradarśakam

mahārāgaṃ namaskṛtya padmanarteśvaraṃ prabhum|iha stokaṃ pravakṣyāmi svacittapratyavekṣaṇāt

yogācārasya nayataḥ sarvameva suniścitam|tatsarvamiha vaktavyaṃ tasmādevatsamācaret

]yena yena hi bandhyante jantavo raudrakarmaṇā|sopāyena tu tenaiva mucyante bhavabandhanāt

[viśuddhereva sattvasya viśuddhaṃ jāyate phalam]|mahāyāne suvispaṣṭamuktametatsuvistaram

dharmapudgalanairātmyaṃ cittamātraṃ jagau muniḥ|tato'pi sarvamutpannamāgamātyanukūlakam

bhāvagrāhagrahāveśagṛhītānpraticoditaḥ|āgame'pi hi suvyakto vistaraḥ karuṇātmanā

manaḥpūrvaṅgamā dharmā manaḥśreṣṭhā manojavāḥ|manasā hi prasannena bhāṣate vā karoti vā

svapitā bhikṣuṇā vṛddhaḥ śīghraṃ gaccheti preritaḥ|patanācca mṛte tasminnānantaryeṇa yujyate

sumlānenārhatādioṣṭo maṅgalaṃ paripīḍaya|upasthāyakabhikṣuḥ sa mṛte tasminna doṣabhāk

andhasaṃjñayā nāndhāṃstu mārayandoṣamaśrute|ityuktaṃ vinaye vyaktaṃ na doṣo'duṣṭacetasām

na stūpakhanane doṣastatsaṃskāradhiyā yataḥ|kevalaṃ puṇyarāśiḥ syādupānantaryakāraṇāt

upānadyugalaṃ dattvā munermūrdhni śubhāśayāt|apanīya tathā cāndho rājyaṃ phalamavāpnutaḥ

tasmādāśayamūlā hi pāpapuṇyavyavasthitiḥ|ityuktamāgame yasmānnāpattiḥ śubhacetasām

svādhidaivatayogātmā jagadarthakvatodyamaḥ|bhuñjāno viṣayān yogī mucyate na ca lipyate

yathaiva viṣatattvajño viṣamālokya bhakṣayan|kevalaṃ muhyate nāsau rogamuktaśca jāyate

māyāmarīcigandharvanagarasvaprasannibham|jagatsarvaṃ samālokya kiṃ kathaṃ kena bhujyate

bālā rajyanti rūpeṣu vairāgyaṃ yānti madhyamāḥ|svabhāvajñā vimucyante rūpasyottamabuddhayaḥ

vicintya samayaṃ sarvaṃ devatāpūjanāvidhim|śuddhamālokya niḥśaṅkaṃ bhoktavyaṃ mantracoditam

śodhyaṃ bodhyaṃ tathā dīpyamakṣaratrayayogataḥ|aṅguṣṭhānāmikāgrābhyāṃ prīṇayecca tathāgatān

yatsatyamiti bālānāṃ tanmithyā khalu yoginām|gacchannantamanenaiva na baddho na ca mucyate

saṃsāraṃ caiva nirvāṇaṃ manyante'tattvadarśinaḥ|na saṃsāraṃ na nirvāṇaṃ manyante'tattvadarśinaḥ

vikalpo hi mahāgrāhaḥ saṃsārodadhipātakaḥ|avikalpā mahātmāno mucyante bhavabandhanāt

śaṅkāviṣeṇa bādhyante viṣeṇeva pṛthagjanāḥ|tāmevotkhātya nirmūlaṃ vicaretkaruṇātmakaḥ

yathaiva sphaṭikaḥ svacchaḥ pararāgeṇa rajyate|tathaiva cittaratnaṃ tu kalpanārāgarañjitam

prakṛtyā kalpanārāgairviviktaṃ cittaratnakam|ādiśuddhamanutpannaṃ niḥsvabhāvamanāvilam

tattadyatnena kartavyaṃ yadyadbālairvigarhitam|svādhidaivatayogena cittanirmalakāraṇāt

rāgāgniviṣasaṃmugdhā yogināṃ śubhacetasā|kāmitāḥ khalu kāminyaḥ kāmamokṣaphalāvahāḥ

yathā svagarūḍaṃ dhyātvā viṣamākṛṣya saṃpiban|karoti nirviṣaṃ sādhyaṃ na viṣeṇābhibhūyate

dvādaśayojanavyāsaṃ cakraṃ vai śirasi bhramat|bodhicittaṃ samutpādya apanītamiti śrutiḥ

bodhicittaṃ samutpādya sambodhau kṛtacetasā|tatrāsti yanna kartavyaṃ jagadudvaraṇāśayā

ādiśuddhamanutpannaṃ niḥsvabhāvamanāvilam|jagadbhāvena sampaśyanna baddho na ca mucyate

vicintya vidhivadyogī devatāguṇavistaram|rajyate rāgacittena rāgabhogeṇa mucyate

kiṃ kurmaḥ kutra vai labhyā vicitrā bhāvaśaktayaḥ|viṣākrānto yathā kaścidviṣeṇaiva tu nirviṣaḥ

karṇājjalaṃ jalenaiva kaṇṭakenaiva kaṇṭakam|rāgeṇaiva tathārāgamudvaranti maṇīṣiṇaḥ

yathaiva rajako vastraṃ malenaiva tu nirmalam|kuryādvijñastathātmānaṃ malenaiva tu nirmalam

yathā bhavati saṃśuddho rajonirghṛṣṭadarpaṇaḥ|sevitastu tathā vijñairdoṣo doṣavināśanaḥ

lohapiṇḍo jale kṣipto majjatyeva tu kevalam|pātrīkṛto sa evāndhaṃ tārayettarati svayam

tavdatpātrīkṛtaṃ cittaṃ prajñopāyavidhānataḥ|bhuñjāno mucyate kāmo mocayatyaparānapi

durvijñaiḥ sevitaḥ kāmaḥ kāmo bhavati bandhanam|sa eva sevito vijñaiḥ kāmo mokṣaprasādhakaḥ

prasiddhaṃ sakale loke kṣīraṃ viṣavināśanam|tadeva phaṇibhiḥ pītaṃ sutarāṃ viṣavardhanam

jale kṣīraṃ yathāviṣṭaṃ haṃso pibati paṇḍitaḥ|saviṣān viṣāyāṃstadvad bhuktvā muktaśca paṇḍitaḥ

yathaiva vidhivadbhuktaṃ viṣamapyamṛtāyate|durbhuktaṃ ghṛtapūrādi bālānāntu viṣāyate

idameva hi yaccittaṃ śodhitaṃ hetubhiḥ śubhaiḥ|nirvikalpaṃ nirālambaṃ bhāti prakṛtinirmalam

yathā vahniḥ kṛśopyeṣa tailavartyādisaṃskṛtaḥ|dīpo nirmalaniṣkampaḥ sthirastimiranāśanaḥ

vaṭabījaṃ yathā sūkṣmaṃ sahakārasamanvitam|śākhāmūlaphalopetaṃ mahāvṛkṣavidhāyakam

haridrācūrṇasaṃyogādvarṇāntaramiti smṛtam|prajñopāyasamāyogāddharmadhātuṃ tathā viduḥ

ghṛtaṃ ca madhusaṃyuktaṃ samāṃśaṃ viṣatāṃ vrajet|tadeva vidhivadbhuiktamutkṛṣṭaṃ tu rasāyanam

rasaghṛṣṭaṃ yathā tāmraṃ nirdoṣaṃ kāñcanaṃ bhavet|jñānaśuddhyā tathā kleśāḥ samyak kalyāṇakārakāḥ

hīnayānābhirūḍhānāṃ mṛtyuśaṅkā pade pade|saṃgrāmajayacittastu dūra eva vyavasthitaḥ

mahāyānābhirūḍhastu karuṇādharmavarmitaḥ|prajñātantudhanurbāṇo jagaduḍvaraṇāśayaḥ

mahāsattvo mahopāyaḥ sthirabudviratandritaḥ|jitvā dustarasaṅgrāmaṃ tārayedaparānapi

paśavo'pi hi kliśyante svārthamātraparāyaṇāḥ|jagadarthavidhātāro dhanyāste viralā janāḥ

śītavātādiduḥkhāni sahante svārthalampaṭāḥ|jagadarthapravṛttāste na sahante kathaṃ nu te

nārakāṇyapi duḥkhāni soḍhavyāni kṛpālubhiḥ|śītavātādiduḥkhāni kastānyapi vicārayet

na kaṣṭakalpanāṃ kuryānnopavāsena ca kriyām|snānaṃ śaucaṃ na caivātra grāmadharmaṃ vivarjayet

nakhadantāsthimajjānaḥ pituḥ śukravikārajāḥ|māṃsaśoṇitakeśādi mātṛśoṇitasambhavam

itthamaśucisambhūtaḥ piṇḍo yo'śucipūritaḥ|kathaṃ saṃstādṛśaḥ kāyo gaṅgāsnānena śudhyati

na hyaśucirghaṭastoyaiḥ kṣālito'pi punaḥ punaḥ|tadvadaśucisampūrṇaḥ piṇḍo'pi na viśudhyati

pratarannapi gaṅgāyāṃ naiva śvā śuddhimarhati|taddadvarmadhiyāṃ puṃsāṃ tīrthasnānaṃ tu niṣphalam

dharmo yadi bhavetsnānātkaivartānāṃ kṛtārthatā|naktandivaṃ jalasthānāṃ matsyādīnāṃ tu kā kathā

pāpakṣayo'pi snānena naiva syāditi niścayaḥ|yato rāgādivṛddhistu dṛśyate tīrthasevinām

rāgo dveṣaśca mohaśca īrṣyā tṛṣṇā ca sarvadā|pāpānāṃ mūlamākhyātaṃ naiṣāṃ snānena śodhanam

ātmātmīyagrahādete sambhavantīha janminaḥ|avidyāhetukaḥ so'pi sāvidyā bhrāntiriṣyate

raupyabuddhiryathā śuktau śuktidṛṣṭau nivartate|nairātmyadarśanātsāpi nirmūlamavasīdati

sarpabuddhiryathā rajjau rajjudṛṣṭau nivartate|sarpabuddhiḥ punastatra naiva syādiha janmani

sattvabuddhistathātrāpi vajrajñānānnivartate|na bhāvaḥ sambhavettatra dagdhabīja ivāṅkuraḥ

nairātmyaśucisaṅgātaḥ piṇḍaḥ prakṛtinirmalaḥ|tasya santāpane dharmaḥ kaṣṭaṃ bālairvikalpitaḥ

candrodayavyayañjāpi apekṣya tithikalpanā|sūryodayavyayenāpi divārātrivyavasthitiḥ

pūrvādivyavahāro'pi kalpanāpekṣayā kṛtaḥ|grahanakṣatrarāśyādi sarvalokairvikalpitam

śītoṣṇavarṣaṇāpekṣā tathaiva ṛtukalpanā|svakarmaphalabhogo'yaṃ śubhāśubhagrahoditaḥ

avidyākardamāliptaṃ cittacintāmaṇiṃ pumān|pravṛttaḥ kṣālituṃ vidvān ko'vidyāṃ vṛṃhayetpunaḥ

na grahatithinakṣatradeśakālādyapekṣaṇam|viharennirvikalpastu nirnimittamaśaṅkitaḥ

yadyadindriyamārgatvaṃ yāyāttattatsvabhāvataḥ|susamāhitayogena sarvaṃ buddhamayaṃ vadet

cakṣurvairocano buddhaḥ śravaṇaṃ vajrasūryakaḥ|ghrāṇaṃ ca paramāśvastu padmanarteśvaro mukham

kāyaḥ śrīheruko rājā vajrasattvaśca mānasam|evaṃ samyak sadā yogī vicaretkaruṇātmakaḥ

siddhāntī nirvikalpo'sau sthirakalpastu dhīdhanaḥ|yatheṣṭaceṣṭāvyāpāraḥ sarvabhuk sarvakṛttathā

sarvakāmakriyākārī yathārucitaceṣṭitaḥ|utthito vā niṣaṇo vā caṅkramanvā svapaṃstathā

amaṇḍalapraviṣṭo vā sarvāvaraṇavānapi|svādhidaivatayogātmā mandapuṇyo'pi sidhyati

anena sarvasauritvaṃ sarvabuddhatvameva vā|janmanīhaiva tattvajñaḥ samprāpnoti na saṃśayaḥ

yathā prākṛtalokena yogiloko na bādhyate|bādhyante dhīviśeṣeṇa yogino'pyuttarottaraiḥ

mahāprajñāmahopāyamahākṛpādhimokṣataḥ|mahāyānasamuddiṣṭaṃ mahāsattvasya gocaram

yatkalpānāmasaṃkhyeyairna prāptaṃ bahubhirmatam|janmanyatraiva buddhatvaṃ prāpyate nātra saṃśayaḥ

mahāyānasya māhātmyaṃ puṇyajñānena sambhṛtam|sarvajñatvaṃ padaṃ ramyaṃ sadyo janmani labhyate

āgamaśruticintā tu mahāyāne na gṛhyate|āśayānuśayābhedād yānābhedaḥ prakāśyate

anya evādhimokṣo'yaṃ tathānyā bodhicārikā|anyā cittaviśuddhiśca phalamandhadihocyate

samīpe nirmalādarśe rūpaṃ nirmalacakṣuṣaḥ|yathā bhāti suvispaṣṭaṃ svacchaprakṛtinirmalam

vidhūtakalpanājālaviṣpraṣṭaśuddhacetasāṃ|yogināñca tathā jñānaṃ prajñānirmaladarpaṇaiḥ

sūryakāntisamāśliṣṭasūryakāntamaṇau yathā|sahasā prajvalatyagniḥ samathaḥ svārthasādhane

apāstakalpanājālaṃ sūryakāntanibhaṃ manaḥ|prajñāsūryāṃ śusaṃśliṣṭaṃ tadvajjvalati yoginām

kāṣṭhadvayanigharṣeṇa yathā jvalati pāvakaḥ|ādimadhyāntasaṃśuddhaḥ sarvavastuprakāśakaḥ|prajñopāyasamāyogādyogijñānaṃ
tathā viduḥ

yathaivaikaḥ pradīpo'yaṃ varttyantarasamāśritaḥ|yathāsvārthaṃ yathāsthānaṃ karotyuccaiḥ prakāśanam

sphuraṇānantamūrtistu prajñopāyavibhāvanaiḥ|nānādhimuktasattvānāṃ yathākṛtyamanuṣṭhayet

vidhi jñohi yathā kaścitkṣīrādamṛtamuddharet|nirdoṣaṃ śītalaṃ hṛdyaṃ sarvavyādhivināśanam

prajñākṣīramahopāyādvidhivanmathanotthitaḥ|viśuddhadharmadhātuḥ sa sukhāsukhavināśanaḥ

yathā latā samudbhūtā phalapuṣpasamanvitā|tathaikakṣaṇasambodhiḥ sambhāradvayasaṃyutā

[vaśadveṣagatistambha] varṣaṇākarṣaṇādikam|madyamāṃsarato yogī kurvannāpyupalipyate

[hastakaṅkaṇabimbāya ki] mādarśaḥ samīkṣyate|mahāyāne yato'dyāpi mantrasāmarthyadarśanam

mātṛduhitṛsambandha [stattvato'tra na kalpyate|bhagnāyodhūpavarttīva] jagadāha tathāgataḥ

pañcabhūtātmakaṃ śukraṃ śoṇitañcāpi tādṛśam|tanmayaḥ khalu piṇḍo'yaṃ ko vipraḥ kaśca vāntyajaḥ

[pañcaskandhātmakaṃ sarvaṃ] śarīraṃ khalu bhikṣavaḥ|anityaṃ duḥkhaśūnyañca na jātirna ca jātimān

kaivarttīgarbhambhūtaḥ kaściñcā [ṇḍālajātimān |tapasā brāhmaṇo jātastasmājjātirakāraṇam

svasāraṃ mātaraṃ śvaśrūṃ svaputrīṃ bhāgineyikām|brāhmaṇīṃ kṣatriyāṃ vaiśyāṃ vidhijñānena śūdrikām]

ekāṅgavikalāṃ hīnāṃ garhītāmantyajāmapi|yoṣitaṃ pūjayennityaṃ jñānavajraprabhāvanaiḥ

[sarvadā smitavakreṇa mantravistṛtacakṣuṣā|sambodhau cittamutpādya svādhidaivatabhāvataḥ

paśyeddṛśyaṃ kṣaṇaṃ kiñcicchotavyaṃ śṛṇuyāttathā|satyāsatyaviyuktaṃ tu vadedvākyamatandritaḥ]

snānābhyañjanavastrādikhānapānādiyantataḥ|svādhidaivatayogena cintayetpūjanāvidhim

[gītaṃ vādyaṃ tathā nṛtyaṃ sopāyena vratī bhajet|akurvanniha bhāveṣu sarveṣvabhiniveśanam

svātmabhāvaprahāṇena tāpayenna tapasyayā]|sukhādyathā sukhaṃ dhyāyetsambuddho'yamanāgataḥ

sarvakāmopabhogaistu ramatha muktito'bhayāt|mā bhaiṣṭa nāsti vaḥ pāpaṃ samayo duratikramaḥ

mantrasaṃskṛtakāṣṭhādi devatvamadhigacchati|kiṃ punaḥ jñānavān kāyaḥ kaṣṭaṃ mohaviceṣṭitam

prākṛtatvamahaṅkāraṃ parityajya samāhitaḥ|prajñopāyavidhānena kriyāmimāṃ samācaret

paṅkajātaṃ yathā padmaṃ paṅkadoṣairna lipyate|vikalpavāsanādoṣaistathā yogī na lipyate

[vikalpo vimbasaṅkāśo dṛṣṭidoṣairna lipyate|anbhasā lipyate naiva yadvadudakacandramāḥ]

anādivāsanāpaṅkairviliptaṃ cittaratnakam|prajñopāyajalenaiva [kṣālitaṃ samprakāśate]

svādhidevatayogasya sthiracittasya dhīmataḥ|muktaḥ kudṛṣṭimeghaiśca bhāsate cittabhāskaraḥ

[prajñālakṣmaparicchede bhūtārthasya viniścayāt|dharmadhāturupādeyo'vidyāvyatyayavarjanāt

prajñāmudgaravidhvaste] sahasā kalpanāghaṭe|prakṛtyā nirmalaḥ svaccho jñānadīpaḥ prakāśate

suprasiddhāni bhūtāni kṣityagnijalavāyavaḥ|kriyante hyanyathā vijñairmantrasāmarthyayogataḥ

sarvavādaṃ parityajya mantravādaṃ samācaret|yasya mantrasya sāmarthyātsaukhyabhāvo'pi sidhyati

triratnaṃ na parityājyaṃ bodhicittaṃ tathā guruḥ|na vadhyāḥ prāṇinaḥ ke'pi samayānyapyadhiṣṭhayet

madhu raktaṃ sakarpūraṃ raktacandanayojitam|munivajrodakaṃ caiva pañcaitānyapyadhiṣṭhayet

anyaiśca samayairdivyaiścittasyotkarṣakārakaiḥ|mārutakṣobhaśāntyarthaṃ prīṇayeccittavajrakam

[nāśucibhāva āśaṅkyo'vikalpyayogalīlayā|samāyuktena cittena mantrī sarvaṃ samācaret]

makṣikāpadamātreṇa viṣeṇāpyabhibhūyate|aṇumātrā ghṛṇā śaṅkā mṛtyukaṣṭena saṃyutā

suyuddhaṃ vācaredvijñaḥ supalāyanameva vā|āntarālikabhāvastu vyartho vai patanaṃ bhavet

gurorājñāñca mudrāñca chāyāmapi na laṅghayet|guṇāstasya paraṃ grāhyā doṣā naiva kadācana

ācāryaḥ paramo devaḥ pūjanīyaḥ prayatnataḥ|svayaṃ vajradharo rājā sākṣādrūpeṇa saṃsthitaḥ

yathodakamaṇiḥ śuddhaḥ kaluṣodakaśodhakaḥ|śraddhāmaṇistathā proktaścittaratnaviśodhakaḥ

śraddhāvānmuhyate ko'pi prajñācakṣurvivarjitaḥ|utpādayedataḥ prajñāmāgamādhigamātmikām

śrāddho bahuśrutaḥ prājñaḥ prakṛtyā karuṇātmakaḥ|jagadduḥkhavināśāya sukhopāyaṃ sa vindati

cittaviśuddhimādhāya yanmayopārjitaṃ sukham|cittaviśuddhimādhāya tenāstu sukhito janaḥ
